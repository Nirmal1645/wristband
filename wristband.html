Safety That Works Without a Phone

The Safeguard wristband provides immediate emergency response directly through cellular networks. No smartphone required, no app needed. Just wear it and feel secure.

How Safeguard Protects You

Multiple activation methods ensure help is always within reach

Independent Cellular Connection

Built-in GSM/LTE-M/NB-IoT module sends SOS alerts directly without needing a phone or WiFi connection.

Voice Command Activation

Simply say "Start SOS" to trigger an emergency alert. Cancel with "Stop SOS" within 7 seconds.

Gesture Detection

Shake your wrist rapidly 5 times to activate SOS. Sensitivity can be adjusted in settings.

Health Monitoring

Automatic SOS trigger on abnormal heart rate spikes or continuous pressure detection.

GPS Location Tracking

Real-time GPS provides accurate location data to emergency contacts and responders.

Long Battery Life

Advanced power management provides up to 7 days of battery life on a single charge.
